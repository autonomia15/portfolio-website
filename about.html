About

Hi! My name is Mia Shanholtzer. I am an engineer and product designer who works as a freelancer via my company, Autonomia. I have 11 years of experience in product development across a variety of industries, including robotics, consumer products, and medical devices. I work with both startups and large corporations, but regardless of company size, my focus is always on agility and quality.

Product development allows me to combine my passion for creativity, analytical thinking, and strategy. I believe that good products solve a need, but truly great products are also a delight to use.

Bringing a product to market is such a thrilling experience. I feel incredibly lucky to have a profession that affords me creative expression on a daily basis.

If you are working on "the next big thing", let me help translate your vision into a fully realized product.
